User Guide for Weather App

Welcome to the Weather App! This guide will help you understand how to use the application effectively.

1. Searching for a City

  • Navigate to the search bar at the top right corner of the homepage.
  • Type the name of a city (e.g., London, Delhi, New York).
  • Click the Search button or press Enter.

2. Understanding the Data

The weather data displayed includes:

  • Temperature: Current temperature in Celsius.
  • Feels Like: How the temperature actually feels.
  • Humidity: Percentage of humidity in the air.
  • Pressure: Atmospheric pressure in hPa.
  • Visibility: Distance you can see clearly, in meters.
  • Sea Level & Ground Level: Pressure levels measured at sea and ground levels.

3. Viewing Popular Cities

The homepage shows weather data for commonly searched cities like:

  • Boston
  • Lucknow
  • Beijing

4. Troubleshooting

  • If a city is not found, make sure the name is spelled correctly.
  • If the app doesn't respond, check your internet connection.
  • Ensure your browser allows fetching from external APIs.

5. Contact

If you have any feedback or issues, feel free to reach out at support@weatherapp.com.
